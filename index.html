PYBOSSA

Free and Open Source. And priceless

PYBOSSA is the ultimate crowdsourcing framework to analyze or enrich data that can’t be processed by machines alone.

Batteries included!

PyBossa is

a Webserver
a Python library
an Enki statistical analysis library
a WebHook real-time analysis micro-service
tons of templates and examples
free and open source
